Urban Courtyard

The Urban Courtyard was an urban housing concept developed by Meegan Barone and myself, as part of a studio course on urban housing called Jaliangan. the concept prioritises a mix of highly personal and shared spaces and seeks to be a possible solution to the problems posed by increased urban population density, and modern ways of living.

​

The Urban Courtyard accomplishes this by encouraging efficient cooperative living through shared spaces, as well as a personal, highly intimate space. Backing up this design is a logistical storage system designed to maximise convenience and flexibility. This system allows one's belongings to be readily available anywhere in the building. The storage system also intergrates with services both within the building, and those offered by the service economy (ie. Uber, Deliveroo or online marketplaces such as Gumtree and Etsy).
